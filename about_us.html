OUR MISSION

The Hope Foundation is the world's largest non profitable foundation mainly focused on children without parental care and support.Our mission is to provide food, shelter, medical care, educational assistance, adoption advocacy and more in a manner that breaks the cycle of dependence and promotes individual and societal productivity as well as charitable character with the community. We have been working to ensure that children grow up in a loving family environment.
